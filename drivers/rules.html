IJRL Rules of Racing

NOTICE: Please make sure to report your incidents after a race otherwise we can not award correct penalties or remove any wrongly awarded penalties. We are not able to review each perspective for a whole race, as this would take a huge amount of time (all admins are volunteers). We highly recommend that you record your POV during the race or save the full race replay and create clips, so the process is easier for you and for the admins.


General Rules

  • Allowed Assists:
    • Anti-Lock Brakes (ABS)
    • Traction Control (TC)
    • Racing Line
    • Gearbox
    • Pit Release Assist
    • ERS & DRS
  • Banned Assists
    • Pit Entry & Exit Assist (Pit Assist)
    • Braking Assist
    • Steering Assist
  • Current Points System (May change in future seasons)
    • 1st - 15
    • 2nd - 12
    • 3rd - 10
    • 4th - 8
    • 5th - 7
    • 6th - 6
    • 7th - 4
    • 8th - 3
    • 9th - 2
    • 10th - 1
    • Fastest lap (If inside the top 10) - 1
  • Racers in a full-time seat must race at least 85% races and must not miss two consecutive races without explanation, otherwise this may lead to them losing their full-time seat.
  • If you are unable to attend a race and are in a full-time seat, you must make this known in the #ðŸ“‹registration channel for your tier.
  • We reserve the right to remove a driver from the league if they show persistent unsportsmanlike behavior.
  • We reserve the right to remove a driver from their seat if their connection persistently causes issues for other drivers.

  • To receive championship points a racer need not finish the race, but at least 90% of the winner's race distance must be completed.
  • If you receive a time penalty from the game, and you feel this was awarded unfairly, you can make an inquiry for the penalty to be removed in the incident report form for your tier. When making a report, you must provide evidence otherwise we can not judge the situation.
  • A game awarded penalty that has been served during the race (e.g. 5 second hold during pit stop) can not be removed by stewards post race.
  • If you cause an incident then you will be punished in the form of penalty points and/or further time penalties.
  • The penalty point system and a full list of penalties can be found here. This will be a general guidance for our stewards to use.

  • If qualifying has already been completed and a restart is called, then top 10 must choose the tire they originally selected on otherwise you will be disqualified. Everyone else has free choice.
  • If a restart is called on a formation lap then the race will restart without a formation lap.

  • Any form of cheating is strictly forbidden and will end up in a permanent racing and server ban if caught doing so.
  • Drivers must use their discord name as their steam profile name so there is no confusion on who is racing.

Pre Race

  • Full time drivers have to make registration 1h before race starts.
  • Reserves drivers will get seat before race (instead of drivers who haven't done registration and who won't race).
  • Available reserves will be rotated through and put into cars by admins.
  • Full time drivers who haven't done registration will be awarded with 1 penalty point.
  • Reserve driver who hadn't got team will be able to join session if there is free spot 5 minutes before session start.
  • Full time drivers have to tell admins about possibly being late so we will leave a free spot with possibility to join during qualifying.

Qualifying

  • In qualifying, if you have finished your laps and want to retire it is mandatory to retire it in the pits after you finished your lap, otherwise, penalties will be applied.
  • You must make sure not to run out of fuel during qualifying, as this can cause issues for other racers.
  • Impeding cars which are on their qualifying lap will lead to penalties being applied.
  • Please take care when re-joining the track, causing an incident will lead to penalties.
  • If you arrive late to qualifying you will not be able to set a lap. If you do this will lead to penalties being applied.

Race Rules

  • Remember when setting up your race strategy at the start of the race, please do not ready up instantly to allow all drivers to set up!
  • Keep racing clean and avoid contact with other drivers. If you do make contact this will likely result in a penalty for either party, unless it is deemed a racing incident.
  • It is generally accepted that you follow standard F1 rules unless otherwise stated.
  • Resetting your car onto the track is strictly forbidden. Some exceptions may apply in extreme circumstances (e.g.: stuck sideways in the castle section in Baku and unable to get out) otherwise a penalty will be applied.
  • Sitting in AI mode for more than 5 seconds at any point in the race, including particularly a safety car, is strictly forbidden except for extreme circumstances. We do this as this has previously caused issues with the lobby and/or the safety car.
  • Dangerous driving or misconduct during a safety car or formation lap, will lead to penalties points and potentially a time penalty if you disrupt another driver's race.

  • If a driver gets an illegal overtake then please assess the situation and do not come to a sudden stop, if you show that you did try to give the position back but the game gave you a penalty, you can appeal it.

  • When the game shows you the blue flag, you have been caught up by a car on a lap ahead of you. When being lapped, it is your responsibility to let the leaders through safely at the earliest opportunity, without blocking them.
  • If you have been lapped and are clearly faster than a car that is a lap ahead, then you are allowed to unlap yourself. If you cannot gap the car you are unlapping yourself from then you must allow them to have free air.

  • While the race is in progress you will be penalized if you retire the car anywhere on track. If you need to leave the race for any reason it is mandatory to retire the car in the pits, not doing this will cause a penalty.
  • If connection is lost while racing, you are able to join back, failure to rejoin without reason may lead to penalty points.
  • Please take care when re-joining the track, causing an incident will lead to penalties.
  • When a safety car or VSC has been deployed. Drivers must do their best to stick to the delta. Drivers found to be driving unnecessary slow on safety car restarts or during the VSC (for example, being 4+ seconds positive on the delta) may receive penalty points.
  • DRS chicken is forbidden if there are more than 2 cars involved. Doing so will result in dangerous driving and penalties will be applied.

  • RACE WILL BE RESTARTED IF THERE IS 3-4 DNFS BEFORE SECTOR 1 ENDS AND ALSO IF 2 DNFS + 4 OR MORE DRIVERS NEED TO CHANGE WING. RACE WILL ONLY BE RESTARTED TWICE UNLESS SOMETHING THAT AFFECTS THE WHOLE GRID HAPPENS. AND IF ANY MAJOR DESYNC ISSUES ARE FOUND ON THE FIRST LAP.
